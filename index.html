Olympic Performance of Countries

The main goal of this webpage is to provide the general public with a series of visualizations that offer insights into the performance of countries at the Olympic Games.

Users will be able to explore how various factors—such as the number of participating coaches, a country’s GDP, national investment in sports, and the popularity of different sports—can influence a country's performance and trends across various disciplines.

These visualizations are designed for anyone interested in learning more about the Olympics, regardless of their background.

By transforming complex datasets into intuitive visual formats, the webpage aims to make it easier for the general public to identify trends, correlations, and disparities between countries and sports disciplines.

This helps facilitate a quicker and more accessible understanding of Olympic performance, even for those without specialized knowledge.

A performance score is attributed to each country using the formula: Score = 3 × Gold + 2 × Silver + 1 × Bronze.

This visualization presents the performance scores of countries based on their GDP or population for a specific Olympic season. It allows for comparisons between nations of different sizes and continents, revealing how efficiently countries convert resources or population into performance. Patterns may emerge, such as smaller nations outperforming larger ones in efficiency or wealthier countries securing higher medal scores. Users can filter by season and choose between GDP or population as the basis for comparison.

Radar Comparison of Leading Olympic Nations by Socioeconomic and Demographic Indicators

This visualization ranks countries based on their medal scores, using radar charts to compare their demographic and economic profiles. It highlights differences in factors like GDP per capita, youth population, and athlete count, offering insights into how these indicators may influence Olympic success. All variables are normalized on a 1–10 scale for easy visual comparison. Hovering over a country reveals its name and the corresponding values for each indicator.

Olympic Medal Scores by Discipline and Country

This visualization ranks countries based on their medal scores, categorized by Olympic season and discipline. It highlights national strengths in specific sports, such as Alpine Skiing, and offers a quick comparison of medal performance across top-performing countries within the selected category. Users can filter by season and discipline to explore trends and patterns in Olympic performance.

Performance Trends of Top Countries by Olympic Medal Scores

This visualization showcases the top 10 countries ranked by their medal scores, tracking performance trends across Olympic years. It highlights how national medal totals have evolved over time, revealing patterns such as consistent dominance or emerging competitors. Users can filter by season to explore trends in Summer, Winter, or both Olympic Games.
